Description

The "Early 2007" colored iPod shuffle (Second Generation) models are identical to the iPod shuffle (Second Generation) except that they are available in colors -- orange, green, blue, and pink -- as well as the original silver. However, regardless of color, the colored iPod shuffle (Second Generation) models ship with revised earbuds that are designed to sit in the ear without foam "caps" -- previously introduced with the iPod 5th Generation Enhanced and iPod nano 2nd Generation -- and the packaging uses gray text instead of green.
